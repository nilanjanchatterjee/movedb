Methods

We try to update the map regularly with the updated data, but if you think any study are missing then please feel free to add your data here with the column headers,
"Title" : Title of the study
"DOI" : Digital Object Identifier of the study
"Publication Year": Year of Publication
"Sample Size" : Number of Tagged Individuals
"Location" : Name of the study location
"species" : Latin name of the study species
"Latitude" : Latitude of the study location
"Longitude" : Longitude of the study location
